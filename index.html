ABOUT THE EP

"A Crack Between Reality and Emotion" explores the space between our logical understanding of the world and our emotional responses to it. This EP represents a journey through different states of mind, from introspection to revelation, creating a sonic landscape that bridges the gap between what we think and what we feel.
